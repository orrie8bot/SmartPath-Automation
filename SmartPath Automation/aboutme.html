About SmartPath Automation

SmartPath Automation History

SmartPath Automation was launched in September 2020, by owner/operator Douglas Webber. SmartPath Automation is based out of the Greenville/Spartanburg area in South Carolina. SmartPath Automation prides themselves on being highly organized and customer-focused Automation Integration Specialist. Over 10 years of experience with Robotic Programming with ABB, Fanuc and Kawasaki. Experienced applications include material handling, sealing, mig welding, spot welding, and painting. Excellent communication skills, ability to build robust and mutually beneficial business relationships at all levels.
